About us

What are we?

We are a multination organisation that provide the link between the job seekers and employers

We take pride in taking through the journey by giving you career advice, your curriculum vitae review and helping build your confidance through mock interviews and cognitive assesment tests

Our past

Our organisation was born in 2014 out of the need for career couching and since then we've diversified to be among the top organisations for job advertising, career guidance with a large networks to the well known world employers. Originally our engagement was a one on one but since we started using this platform the communit has trippled

we can proudly confirm that over 580, 976 candidates successfully landed their job application through this platform and our affiliation has grown to over 700 employer. Join us and be part of the community

Job Advertisement

The main goal of a job advertisement is to inform potential job candidates about a new opening and attract them to apply. It is written in an engaging tone and it contains information not only about the job position, but also about your company and the benefits you offer.

CV/Resume Review

A CV review will give you the professional feedback you don't get from HR managers. The personalised critique will help you know if your current CV is ready to land you your dream job or will hold you back. It's ensure that you are truly putting your best foot forward

Job Search Advice

There is a great deal to learn when it comes to pursuing and excelling in your career. It can be helpful to read and listen to career advice and inspire you to do something else or be kept in the back of your mind for another day when you truly need it.

Cognitive ability assesment

We test you for job-specific skills like coding or digital marketing, as well as more general skills like critical thinking. Our unique personality and value tests allow you to get to know you as real people – not just pieces of paper.
